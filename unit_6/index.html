Concentric Learning

Have something to teach? Why not tailor it to the world's biggest classroom?

What we do

We provide end-to-end course development services including learner analysis, instructional design, storyboarding, scripting, online development, and quality assurance. We understand that online learning is not a one-size-fits-all situation. We tailor our solutions to the needs of each client and ultimately to the needs of each student.

Who we work with

We work with higher education institutions worldwide as well as publishers, education companies, and anybody with something that they want to teach.

Why they work with us

A BOUTIQUE SOLUTION

Concentric Learning offers the depth of experience of a larger company with the consultative approach and attention of a boutique firm.

A MULTI -DISCIPLINARY TEAM

We create learning experiences that are engaging, effective and enjoyable by combining the experience of a learning designer with the talents of a graphic design and user experience expert.

IN-HOUSE CAPABILITY

Our core team can design and build your course in-house, providing a seamless process and team continuity from creative vision through to final delivery.

CUSTOMISED SOLUTIONS

We do not believe educational solutions ever come in one-size-fits-all packages and will work with your experts to ensure they are able to tell their unique story online as well as they do in the classroom.

Our approach is:

  • Collaborative and consultative
  • Strategic
  • Cost-effective
  • Creative
  • Clean and contemporary
  • Driven by the client's vision

Concentric Learning was built on the philosophy that education is more than just access to information. It is the satisfaction and nurturing of one of our greatest human assets: curiosity. Teaching one another is how we grow as a society and we have a responsibility to do it well. Concentric Learning approaches every project from the standpoint of making the learning clear, accessible and engaging. We endeavour to help our partners take full advantage of the available technology to create a transformative online learning experience.
